# 20 atomic commits

- the whole idea of atomic commits is to make small manageable changes
- this way if something goes wrong you can easily change just the part that isn't working
- when you separate your commits you make rewinding the commits a lot more easier
- atomic commit => how to group changes, make small commits when possible
- focus your commits on single concepts
- improve collaboration and branch management
- easier to find bugs, disciplined development habits help
- if other edits are needed, divide and conquer
- make your commits topical and focused
- be mindful when making edits to whitespace characters - spaces, tabs, line returns, they can be easy overlooked and cause the system to show false positives
- beware that very often white space differences are reported in Git
